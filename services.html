South India Tours

FF tours offer a large variety of South India tours. Scroll through the list of tour packages below, and you will find tour packages covering almost every important destination in Southern India. Southern India is famed for it’s temples, it’s architecture and it’s rich cultural heritage. We have several tour packages that include Southern India’s best known tourism destinations like Chennai, Mahabalipuram, Mysore etc., as well as several of Southern India’s lesser known but equally rewarding destinations.

Kerala Tours

FF tours offer a large variety of Kerala tour packages. Scroll through the list of tour packages below, and you will find tour packages covering almost all of Kerala, India’s southern most state, and also India’s second most important tourist destination after Rajasthan. Our range of Kerala holiday packages include the cultural heritage city of Cochin, the backwater retreats of Kumarakom and Alleppey, the Spice plantations of Periyar, the Tea gardens of Munnar and the popular beach destinations of Kovalam, Marari and Bekal. We also have homestay based tours of Kerala, for those who want an authentic experience of what is known as “God’s own country”.
